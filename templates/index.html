Sick Day Rules
  • 1. Never stop insulin
  • 2. Check blood glucose every 2-4 hours
  • 3. Check ketones if blood glucose > 11 mmol/L
  • 4. Stay hydrated - drink sugar-free fluids
  • 5. Seek immediate medical attention if:
    • Persistent vomiting
    • Unable to keep fluids down
    • High ketones
    • Severe abdominal pain
